Bun Bo Hue

Source: BÚN BÒ HUẾ VIETNAMESE SPICY BEEF NOODLES SOUP RECIPE & VIDEO https://seonkyounglongest.com/bun-bo-hue/

Spicy Beef and Pork Noodle Galor

Bun Bo Hue is one of THE best Vietnamese soups that have been created. It has a nice rich broth taste to it, and some spice but nothing too spicy. It's packed full of flavor and mixture of both beef and pork, alongside the aroma of lemon grass.

Like most broths, making it from scratch takes a good amount of patience, but it will all be worth it in the end. This Bun Bo Hue recipe is one that you can make in a shorter amount of time compared to the traditional method.

Ingredients:

  • Beef shank
  • Pork hocks
  • 1-2 cans of chicken broth
  • Any other beef or pork you want to add
  • Lemongrass
  • Bun Giang Tay
  • Crushed dried thai chile
  • 2-3 White Onions
  • Fish sauce
  • Salt and pepper

Optional:

  • Ox Tail (more flavor)
  • Chopped green onion
  • Chopped jalapeno peppers
  • Chopped serrano peppers
  • Pork Blood

Steps:

  1. Cut (not all the way through) the white onion into quarters and place over an open fire/roast them. This will release the aroma and flavors into the soup.
  2. Fill a large pot a little above halfway with water and bring to a boil. Leave enough room for can of broth and meat.
  3. Salt the water and drop in beef bones and any thick cut of beef you chose to include. Boil for 25 minutes.
  4. Clean the water of any residue floating at the top every 5 minutes.
  5. After 25 minutes, add the white onions and bring down to medium heat. Let it sit for 5 minutes.
  6. Once 5 minutes are up, bring heat to medium-high.
  7. Add in cans of chicken broth.
  8. Add in thai chile and lemongrass.
  9. Add more fish sauce and salt to the broth to meet flavor needs. Let sit at medium heat for 10 more minutes.
  10. In another pot, boil some water for the noodles.
  11. Place noodles in the boiling pot and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking and burning at the bottom.
  12. Once finished, strain the noodles and wash with cool water.
  13. Place noodles into bowl, pour hot broth and meat in the bowl to your liking.
  14. Enjoy!
